Report

As we can see in the following graph, we can observe how electric vehicles are distributed in the state of Washington in different cities. Clearly, Seattle stands out with a significantly notable percentage of electric vehicles compared to the others. This graph allows us to compare the number of registered vehicles in each city. The graph indicates that, unlike other cities, Seattle is taking the lead in implementing electric vehicles for daily use, as gasoline vehicles contribute significantly to environmental pollution, while electric vehicles help mitigate this environmental impact. Another factor that likely contributes to this significant difference with Seattle is probably the purchasing power of other cities. As mentioned earlier, Tesla models are the most dominant, being more modern than their competitors, but this does not change the fact that Tesla cars are quite expensive and not everyone has the financial capacity to acquire this type of vehicle. To conclude, it can be said that electric cars are a relatively new technology. In this graph, the percentage of people who still prefer gasoline vehicles is not ruled out, as these vehicles can be more durable and reliable. Although the graph shows that electric cars are more dominant in Seattle, it does not mean that electric vehicles prevail over gasoline vehicles in Seattle. e.

Report

In the following graph, we can observe the distribution of different electric vehicle brands in the state of Washington. As mentioned earlier, it is no coincidence that Tesla dominates as the most predominant electric vehicle brand.

This is due to Tesla's vehicles having autonomous driving capabilities, meaning they can drive themselves. While Tesla vehicles are not cheap, the various features they offer justify their price.

On the other hand, other brands do not hold as significant a percentage, posing no real challenge to Tesla in the market. These brands need to identify what Tesla is doing differently in order to capture a larger share and increase competitiveness.

It is also worth noting that the brands present, aside from Tesla, do not focus on creating electric vehicles; rather, they are focused on other niches and view electric vehicles as a secondary market opportunity.electric vehicle market.
